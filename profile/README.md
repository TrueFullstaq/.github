## About Us

TrueFullstaq is born from the merger of **True** (est. 2000) and **Fullstaq** (est. 2016) — two Dutch internet and cloud pioneers who joined forces in 2024. We design, build, and manage cloud native platforms so your teams can focus on shipping great software.

We were the **first certified Kubernetes organization in the Netherlands**, and today our 170+ engineers help organizations across every stage of their cloud native journey — from first migration to fully managed production workloads.

TrueFullstaq is part of [**The Digital Neighborhood**](https://www.thedigitalneighborhood.com/), a collective of specialist tech companies providing deep technical expertise under one roof.
